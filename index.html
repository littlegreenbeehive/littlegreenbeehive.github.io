meet the worker bees

our philosophy

We started little green beehive after combining Josie’s passion for bees and Nikki’s passion for cosmetics and skin care. We believe in good quality affordable skin care that is based on the properties of the nature around us.

locally sourced

All of our beeswax comes from Josie’s beehives in Kenilworth, Warwickshire, where queen bees Ali, Marlene & Bernadette currently reign. Every year their worker bees produce honeycomb, from which honey is extracted, allowing the wax to be filtered & used in the products.
